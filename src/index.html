How It Works

The process of selling your car with Cardeal is easy and straightforward. Our employees will keep you informed so you will understand what happens next.

Step 1

Fill out a form that provides accurate information about your car (VIN, year, make, model, mileage, condition, accidents).

Step 2

Get an instant offer on your car that is good for 7 days.

Step 3

Provide access to your car. Deliver it to the closest Cardeal location or have us come get it.

Step 4

Provide the title proving your ownership of the vehicle.

Step 5

Cardeal will inspect your car to verify its condition.

Step 6

If everything checks out, you can decide to accept the offer. Choose cash or apply the money to a trade-in.
